How to Set Your Homepage

Follow these simple instructions for your browser

Chrome Instructions

  • Step 1: Click the three dots in the upper right corner
  • Step 2: Click 'Settings'
  • Step 3: Under 'On startup', select 'Open a specific page or set of pages'
  • Step 4: Click 'Add a new page', enter the URL you copied, and click 'Add'

Firefox Instructions

  • Step 1: Click the three lines in the upper right corner
  • Step 2: Click 'Options' or 'Preferences'
  • Step 3: Under 'Home Page', paste the URL you copied

Safari Instructions

  • Step 1: Click on 'Safari' in the top menu bar
  • Step 2: Select 'Preferences'
  • Step 3: In the 'General' tab, set 'Homepage' to the URL you copied

Edge Instructions

  • Step 1: Click the three dots in the upper right corner
  • Step 2: Click 'Settings'
  • Step 3: Under 'Start, home, and new tabs', set your homepage to the URL you copied
